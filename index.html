✿ Project 04

HealthLens Lab

An AI-powered Medical Report Explainer that lets you upload or paste any lab report and get instant clinical-grade analysis. Auto-detects report type, checks every value against normal ranges, and generates an overall health score across Heart, Blood, Diabetes, Liver and Kidney categories. Supports 12 languages including Hindi & Gujarati, generates personalised doctor questions, flags medication conflicts, and recommends the right specialist — all 100% private, zero data stored. ⚕️

🤖 Groq API🧠 Llama 3.3-70B🌐 12 Languages💊 Med Analysis📊 Health Score🔒 100% Private

✿ Project 05

TripArchitect

Your AI-powered personal travel planner! Enter any destination, travel dates, budget and trip style — and Gemini AI crafts a complete day-by-day itinerary with real place names, morning-to-evening activities and local secrets. Features live 7-day weather forecasts, an interactive Leaflet map, a 12-language phrasebook, smart AI-personalised packing lists, budget estimators, emergency contacts and local festival info — all across 9 beautiful tabs. Plan trips to 195+ countries, completely free. 🌍
